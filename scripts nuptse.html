EXT. LONDON SCHOOLYARD - DAY
The scene unfolds against the backdrop of London, brick houses lining the streets. Our BOY strolls down the lane, different – no Nuptse, no swagger. Just an invitation for aggro. As he approaches the schoolyard, he walks past a group of student outside the schoolyard, each sporting a Nuptse like an armor. Their HEAD HONCHO, spotting our BOY, narrows his eyes and yells.

HEAD HONCHO

Oi, mate. You lost? Dressed like that, you're a right walking target bro. Where's your protection?

BOY WITHOUT JACKET

Protection against the cold? It's just a bit of chill, innit?

HEAD HONCHO

No, fam! It ain't about the frost; it's the wolves you gotta watch, the bullies! You’re wearing an invitation to an ass-kicking going to school looking like that.

The HEAD HONCHO pauses, taking a deep breath as he surveys the schoolyard, he rubs the back of his neck, a gesture of annoyance then locks eyes with our boy.

HEAD HONCHO (CONT'D)

Ah man, I don't know why I'm doing this, but imma help you out, bro. At this exact moment, you’re a bully’s wet dream. Imagine if a bully sees you without a Nuptse...Look.

CUT TO:
A Guy Ritchie-style montage, the camera whipping between subjects Quick cuts and slick transitions parade the Nuptse jacket in all its glory as the honcho continues.

HEAD HONCHO (V.O. OUT OF FRAME)

Guy with Nuptse Jacket, guy with Nuptse Jacket, guy without, guy with Nuptse...

Celebrating the diversity in style only possible with a nupts the camera captures a TEENAGER in a Nuptse, brimming with confidence as he strides across the frame. Another leans casually against a wall, his glasses catching the light. Then a group, all in Nuptse jackets. Suddenly, the frame rests on a lone figure, without a Nuptse, a stark contrast to the rest. Lastly a student decked in nuptse but mountain gear styled. As the montage concludes, we return to the group

HEAD HONCHO (CONT'D)

Who do you think he’s going to bully?

BOY

The guy with glasses? Ehh.. Mountain-climber-guy? That seems like an odd identity, worthy of bullying, no?

HEAD HONCHO

Let me provide you insight into the mindset of a bully, bro; a bloke with glasses will always have glasses. But you, on the other hand, might not wear that outfit again. And mountain-guy is the goat, that’s a vintage Nuptse, bro.

The BOY, now aware of the stakes, feels the weight of his vulnerability. Sensing his nervousness the Honcho responds with a hint satisfaction in his voice

HEAD HONCHO

Don’t worry bro. I got your back.

In a gesture of solidarity, the HEAD HONCHO throws the BOY a Nuptse jacket in its iconic pocketed form. Now united by Nuptsies the group enters the schoolyard, ready to face whatever comes their way.

Copy: North Face. We got your back.
